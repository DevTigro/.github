# Tigro

Tigro is a software company based in Olovo, Bosnia and Herzegovina. We partner with companies to design and build modern software and AI solutions, while developing products of our own.

## [Vocaless](https://vocaless.app)

Vocaless gives musicians a simpler way to prepare backing tracks. Remove vocals, change the key or tempo, and organize tracks into playlists for rehearsals and performances.
Pay per track, no subscription
